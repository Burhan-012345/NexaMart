Ordering Questions

How do I place an order? +
To place an order, simply browse our products, add items to your cart, and proceed to checkout. You'll need to create an account or log in, enter your shipping address, select a payment method, and confirm your order.
Can I modify or cancel my order? +
You can modify or cancel your order within 1 hour of placing it. After that, the order enters processing and cannot be changed. Contact our customer service immediately if you need to make changes after the 1-hour window.
How do I use promo codes? +
Enter your promo code in the designated field during checkout. The discount will be automatically applied to your order total. Make sure to check the terms and conditions of the promo code for validity and restrictions.

How can I track my order? +
You can track your order by logging into your account and visiting the 'Order History' section. You'll also receive tracking information via email and SMS once your order is shipped.

We deliver to over 25,000+ pin codes across India. During checkout, enter your pin code to check delivery availability. Some remote areas may have longer delivery times or additional charges.

Returns & Refunds

What is your return policy? +
We offer a 30-day return policy for most items. Products must be unused, in original packaging, and with all tags attached. Some items like perishable goods, intimate apparel, and personalized products cannot be returned.
How long do refunds take? +
Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days after we receive your returned item. The time it takes for the refund to reflect in your account depends on your payment method and bank processing times.
Who pays for return shipping? +
We provide free return shipping for all eligible returns. You'll receive a prepaid return label that you can use to send the item back to us at no cost.

Is it safe to use my credit card? +
Yes, we use industry-standard SSL encryption to protect your payment information. We don't store your complete card details on our servers, ensuring your financial information remains secure.
Why was my payment declined? +
Payment declines can occur due to insufficient funds, incorrect card details, bank security filters, or technical issues. Contact your bank for specific reasons or try an alternative payment method.
